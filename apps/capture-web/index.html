Nonclinical prototype

Observe the conversation, locally.

PhenoMetric derives bounded face and voice engineering measurements during one ambient session of up to five minutes.

No recording or upload

Camera and microphone signals are processed in this browser. Raw media, transcripts, embeddings, and results are not saved.

Before you begin

  • Use a quiet, well-lit space and face the camera.
  • Use headphones if another person’s voice may be audible.
  • This prototype does not verify identity or provide clinical interpretation.

Camera and microphone access requires HTTPS or localhost in Chrome.
